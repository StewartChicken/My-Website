About Me

My name is Evan Poon. I am currently attending the University of Colorado, Boulder, and am pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering, as well as a Traditional Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering. From a very early age, I have found passion in computer hardware, programming, and math. I built my first computer when I was in 10th grade and, after doing so, I found myself fascinated with the complex, underlying construction of each of the various components. I wanted to truly understand how the chips and circuits worked on a fundamental level. My highschool Computer Science and Data Structures classes revealed my love of problem solving through programming as I worked with classmates to create fun projects and games in Java. After taking these classes, I knew that I wanted to pursue a career that pertained to computers, but I wasn’t exactly sure which domain I wished to explore. My love for calculus and differential equations solidified my decision to pursue an academic and professional career in computer engineering, as the field’s combination of math, programming, and electronic design intrigued me like no other.
